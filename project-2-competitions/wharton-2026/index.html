The plan

The Wharton Global Youth Investment Competition runs teams through a simulated portfolio over several weeks, judged partly on returns and partly on the written investment thesis behind the picks. The plan is to apply the same discipline used in Project 1 here — a real thesis and a named risk for every position — and treat this as a chance to defend that reasoning against a panel rather than just a monthly journal.
